Common Questions
When will DokShield launch? +
DokShield is currently in active development with early access available now. We're targeting a full public launch in Q3 2026. Early access users will be first to know — and will receive founding member pricing.
Is Obnet VC-funded? +
No — Obnet is fully independent and self-funded. This means we answer only to our users, not investors. Our roadmap is shaped entirely by real user feedback.
Can I integrate DokShield with my existing tools? +
Yes. DokShield is built API-first, making integration straightforward. It supports SAML 2.0 and OAuth2 for SSO, and provides webhooks for real-time event notifications into your existing systems.
Do you offer an on-premise option? +
On-premise deployment is available on the Enterprise plan. If you have specific infrastructure requirements, get in touch and we'll discuss what's possible for your organization.
How is pricing structured? +
DokShield will offer a free Starter tier, a Professional plan at ~$29/month, and custom Enterprise pricing. Early access users will receive a permanent discount as a thank-you for supporting us early.
Are you open to partnerships? +
Absolutely. If you're a technology provider, consultant, or reseller who sees a fit with our products, we'd love to hear from you. Email us at partners@obnet.org.
