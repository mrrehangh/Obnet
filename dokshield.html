The Problem
Data Centre Security Operations
Are Still Running on Paper.
📋

Fragmented Tools

Shift reports in Word, incidents in email, patrol logs on paper, visitor records in Excel. No single source of truth. No real-time visibility.

⚠️

Compliance Risk

Manual logs are error-prone, incomplete, and difficult to audit. When something goes wrong, the paper trail rarely tells the full story.

🔄

Broken Handovers

Critical information is lost between shifts. The incoming team starts blind. Issues from the previous shift go unresolved for hours.

The Solution
Build Your Platform from 18 Modules.

DokShield is a modular platform — choose only the modules your operation needs and pay only for what you use. Every module is purpose-built, deeply integrated, and designed to be used by real security teams on real shifts. Start with the essentials, expand as you grow.

How It Works
Designed for Real Operational Environments
01

Authenticate

Officers log in by selecting their data centre and colo, then authenticating with their S/O ID. Every session is tied to a specific location and shift.

02

Operate

Access any of the 18 modules from a clean, unified dashboard. Log patrols, manage visitors, raise incidents, and submit shift reports — all in one place.

03

Review

Supervisors and managers get real-time visibility into all operational activity. Dashboards, reports, and audit logs are always current and always accessible.

04

Hand Over

At shift end, DokShield compiles an intelligent handover summary. The incoming team starts fully informed. Nothing is lost between shifts ever again.
