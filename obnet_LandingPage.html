Obnet is an independent software company founded on a simple principle — software should solve real problems, respect the people who use it, and be built to last. No shortcuts. No compromise.

Our commitment: We build for the long term. That means clean architectures, documented systems, and software that grows with its users — not software they grow out of.

Our Journey

How We Got Here

Obnet began with a simple observation: the most critical operational environments — data centres, security operations, compliance-heavy industries — were still being managed with tools that weren't built for them.

We decided to change that. One product at a time.

Q4 2024 — Foundation
Obnet is Born
The company is founded with a clear mission: build precise, reliable, security-first software for teams who can't afford to take risks with their operations. Domain acquired, infrastructure planned, architecture defined.
Q4 2024 — Research & Design
DokShield Concept & Design
After deep research into data centre security operations management — speaking with operators, security officers, and compliance teams — the DokShield concept is defined. A 73-table, 18-module platform designed from the ground up for real operations environments.
Q1 2026 — Development
DokShield Active Development
Full-stack development underway. React + TypeScript frontend, Node.js + Express backend, SQL Server database. The platform is being built module by module, with the Auth and Dashboard foundations in place.
Q3 2026 — Target
DokShield MVP Launch
DokShield targets its first production deployment, initially with Securitas as the pilot client. Early access partners will be first to onboard and will receive founding pricing as a thank-you for their support.
2027 — Growth
Second Product & Expansion
With DokShield stable and growing, Obnet turns its attention to the next problem worth solving in the operational software space. Details to be announced.

The Problem
Data Centre Security Operations
Are Still Running on Paper.
📋

Fragmented Tools

Shift reports in Word, incidents in email, patrol logs on paper, visitor records in Excel. No single source of truth. No real-time visibility.

⚠️

Compliance Risk

Manual logs are error-prone, incomplete, and difficult to audit. When something goes wrong, the paper trail rarely tells the full story.

🔄

Broken Handovers

Critical information is lost between shifts. The incoming team starts blind. Issues from the previous shift go unresolved for hours.

The Solution
Build Your Platform from 18 Modules.

DokShield is a modular platform — choose only the modules your operation needs and pay only for what you use. Every module is purpose-built, deeply integrated, and designed to be used by real security teams on real shifts. Start with the essentials, expand as you grow.

Enterprise Compliance — Built In, Not Bolted On
DokShield is designed from the ground up to meet the world's most demanding security and privacy frameworks.

Immutable audit trails, role-based access control, continuous monitoring, and evidence collection — every control mapped and ready for your annual audit.

GDPR
Privacy by design, right to erasure, data subject request handling, consent management, and cross-border data transfer controls — all built in.

Shift Activity Reports (SAR)

Structured digital shift reports for both Security Officers and Screening Supervisors. Every shift captured, timestamped, and searchable. No more lost handover notes.

Data Hall Occupancy

Real-time occupancy management for data halls and colos. Track who is present, when they entered, and when they left — with full audit history.

Patrols Management

Schedule, track, and verify patrol routes across the facility. Checkpoint logging with timestamp and officer ID. Deviation alerts for missed checkpoints.

Incidents Management

Log, escalate, and resolve incidents with a full audit trail. Severity classification, response tracking, and compliance-ready incident reports at the click of a button.

Visitor Management

Complete visitor lifecycle management — pre-registration, arrival, escort assignment, access zone control, and departure logging. Full compliance trail for every visit.

Issue Resolution & Ticketing

A built-in ticketing system (IRTS) for managing operational issues from creation to resolution. Auto-generated ticket numbers, assignment, priority, and status tracking.

Identity & Access Management

Role-based access control across all modules. Define permissions by role, assign officers to data centres and colos, and maintain a full access history.

Access Control & DCAT

Manage physical access control and DCAT compliance across the facility. Integrated with the visitor and occupancy modules for a unified access picture.

Dashboard & Reports

Real-time operational dashboards for managers and supervisors. Customisable reports for compliance, shift performance, incident trends, and occupancy analytics.

Audit & System Logs

Immutable system-wide audit logs capturing every action taken within DokShield. WHO did WHAT, WHEN, and from WHERE — always available for compliance review.

Supervisor Audit & QA

Quality assurance tools for supervisors to review officer activity, validate patrol completions, and assess shift performance against defined standards.

Shift Handover Intelligence

Intelligent shift handover summaries that automatically compile open incidents, unresolved tickets, patrol status, and critical notes for the incoming team.

Contractor & Vendor Management

Complete lifecycle management for contractors and vendors — onboarding, access approval, work permit tracking, and departure verification.

Emergency & Drill Management

Plan, execute, and document emergency drills and real emergency responses. Evacuation tracking, response time logging, and post-event compliance reporting.

Training & Evaluation

Track officer training records, certifications, and competency evaluations. Ensure your team meets compliance requirements at all times with automated alerts for expiring certifications.

Data Centre Management

Central configuration and management of all data centre assets, zones, colos, and facilities within the platform. The master record for all operational data.

Billing Access

Manage client billing access and subscription tiers across the multi-tenant platform. Role-restricted billing visibility with full audit trail.

Screening Supervisor SAR

Dedicated shift activity reporting for Screening Supervisors — structured, compliant, and integrated with the broader shift handover and audit modules.

How It Works
Designed for Real Operational Environments
01

Authenticate

Officers log in by selecting their data centre and colo, then authenticating with their S/O ID. Every session is tied to a specific location and shift.

02

Operate

Access any of the 18 modules from a clean, unified dashboard. Log patrols, manage visitors, raise incidents, and submit shift reports — all in one place.

03

Review

Supervisors and managers get real-time visibility into all operational activity. Dashboards, reports, and audit logs are always current and always accessible.

04

Hand Over

At shift end, DokShield compiles an intelligent handover summary. The incoming team starts fully informed. Nothing is lost between shifts ever again.

Common Questions
When will DokShield launch? +
DokShield is currently in active development with early access available now. We're targeting a full public launch in Q3 2026. Early access users will be first to know — and will receive founding member pricing.
Is Obnet VC-funded? +
No — Obnet is fully independent and self-funded. This means we answer only to our users, not investors. Our roadmap is shaped entirely by real user feedback.
Can I integrate DokShield with my existing tools? +
Yes. DokShield is built API-first, making integration straightforward. It supports SAML 2.0 and OAuth2 for SSO, and provides webhooks for real-time event notifications into your existing systems.
Do you offer an on-premise option? +
On-premise deployment is available on the Enterprise plan. If you have specific infrastructure requirements, get in touch and we'll discuss what's possible for your organization.
How is pricing structured? +
DokShield will offer a free Starter tier, a Professional plan at ~$29/month, and custom Enterprise pricing. Early access users will receive a permanent discount as a thank-you for supporting us early.
